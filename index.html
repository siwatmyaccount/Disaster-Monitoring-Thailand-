Survival Guide

First Aid

CPR / ปั๊มหัวใจ

Push hard and fast in center of chest. 100-120 beats/min.

Choking / สำลักอาหาร

Perform Heimlich maneuver. Stand behind and wrap arms around waist. Jerk inward and upward.

Snake Bite / งูกัด

Stay still. Keep bite below heart level. DO NOT suck poison.

Disaster Survival

Flood / น้ำท่วม

Turn off electricity. Do not walk near power poles.

Fire / ไฟไหม้

Stay low (crawl). Cover nose/mouth with wet cloth. Use stairs, not elevators.

Electric Shock / ไฟดูด

Do not touch the person. Use wood to move wire. Call 1669.

Earthquake / แผ่นดินไหว

Drop, Cover, and Hold on. Stay away from windows. If outside, find open spot.
